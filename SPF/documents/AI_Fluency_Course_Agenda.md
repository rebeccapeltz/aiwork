# AI Fluency

**Scratch and Peck Feeds**

This course consists of four 1-hour sessions delivered online over four weeks.
The course blends teaching with hands-on interaction with AI. 
Access to a computer and the internet will enable you to interact with AI during the course sessions. 
---

## Week 1: AI Fundamentals and Safe Practices

**Learning Objectives:**

* Understand what AI is and how it works at a basic level
* Understand your role as an AI user
* Learn critical security practices for business use
* Get comfortable with AI interfaces and basic prompting

---

## Week 2: Professional Prompting Frameworks

**Learning Objectives:**

* Master structured prompting for consistent business results
* Create department-specific prompts to generate content
* Understand tone, format, and persona in business contexts

---

## Week 3: Business Productivity and Workflow

**Learning Objectives:**

* Create Product Fact Sheet
* Create practical business documents and communications based on a Fact Sheet
  * Sales
  * Marketing
  * Production
  * Customer Service
  * Cross Functional

---

## Week 4: Problem Solving, Agents, and Data Analysis

**Learning Objectives:**

* Problem Solving with Chain of Thought and Tree of Thought
* Learn about Agents and Create them within a Prompt
* Data Analysis: Create a Dashboard from Raw Data
